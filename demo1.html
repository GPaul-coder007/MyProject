This workshop helps students feel more comfortable with the ACT by building familiarity, clear strategies, and steady practice. We focus on understanding question patterns, improving pacing, and checking answers with confidence.

Our SAT Verbal program supports reading and writing skills through guided practice and clear explanations. Students learn how to approach questions thoughtfully, strengthen comprehension, and express ideas more clearly.
